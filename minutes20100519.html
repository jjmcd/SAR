1 NOTES

The group selected Kent County SOP as the template bylaws to use as the basis for the initial reviews

The plan is to review and create a starter bylaw set for Midland. This will be reviewed by the full preliminary board when it is formed. After some discussion, the group decided to simplify the ByLaws for the organization and spin off another group of documents calling out the "SOP" for the organization. The SOP will call out the details for functional roles, heirarchy, and any neccessary information to maintain operational excellance by the organization. The bylaws will be modeled after the NAMI bylaws, which are currently under final reviews for submittal to the IRS for 501C3 approval.

Emergency Response for health care/medical care will most likely be provided by groups already on the ground in Midland (ERT, etc.). These groups are already staffed and trained and have equipment for this purpose (for example, backpacks with medical supplies available). Future training covering this aspect of rescue can be tackled at a later date.
